# Privacy Policy for MathKit

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## Overview

MathKit is a math learning app designed for children aged 4-11. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with all applicable privacy laws, including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

## Information We Do NOT Collect

MathKit is a **privacy-first** application. We do not:

- ❌ Collect any personal information
- ❌ Track user activity
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Use analytics or advertising services
- ❌ Require account creation or login
- ❌ Access your contacts, location, or camera
- ❌ Store data on external servers

## Data Storage

**All data stays on your device:**

- Quiz progress and scores are stored locally using iOS AsyncStorage
- Achievements and statistics remain on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party
- Data can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

MathKit is designed for children and fully complies with COPPA:

- **No data collection**: We do not collect personal information from children or anyone else
- **No registration required**: Children can use the app without creating an account
- **Offline functionality**: The app works completely offline
- **No in-app purchases**: The app is free with no purchases required
- **No advertisements**: We do not display ads or track users for advertising

## Permissions

MathKit does not request or require any special permissions on your device.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

- Your data is protected by your device's security features
- No data breach risks from external servers
- Complete privacy and control over your information

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
